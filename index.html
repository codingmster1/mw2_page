Modern Warfare Is Back!

Coming To PS4/PS5, XBOX ONE/SERIES X, AND PC October 28!

All New Game Modes and A Thrilling Campaign!

The reveal trailer provided our first look at Modern Warfare 2's campaign in action. We see members of Task Force 141 carrying out a range of operations around the world.From intense action-driven moments to the more stealth-based night missions we've seen in previous entries, a wide variety of combat opportunities were present in the trailer.

Dark Water Level Demo Trailer

A demo of the Dark Water campaign mission was revealed during the Summer Game Fest 2022. taking place on an oil rig in the Gulf of Mexico, this mission showcased Task Force 141 in action as they take control of the site, eliminating any foes that stood in their way.
